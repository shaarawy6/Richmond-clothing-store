Return & Exchange


We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from RICHMOND. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you can exchange it within 7 days of purchase. Please read the following guidelines carefully:


  1. Items must be exchanged in their original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached.
